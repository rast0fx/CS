Section 1 — Moderation Tools

Dyno Bot

Dyno is a primary Discord bot used for various moderation purposes. The United States Air Force Discord utilizes Dyno for managing rule violations.

.warn

Records a rule violation. Must include a clear reason.

  • 2 warnings = 6-hour mute
  • 4 warnings = 24-hour mute
  • 5 warnings = 3-day server ban
  • 8 warnings = Permanent server ban (appealable)
.mute

Temporarily restricts a member from speaking.

Mutes should be used for rule violations that don't warrant a ban but need immediate action.

.kick

Temporarily removes a member. Must include a reason and warning message.

Kicks should be used for repeated offenses or when immediate removal is necessary.

.ban

Permanently removes a member for severe violations. Must include a reason and appeal server link.

Only Senior Moderators or higher may use this command. Bans require approval from at least one other senior staff member.

Section 2 — Server Rules

Low Severity Offenses

  • PG-Friendly Content: Mild profanity or suggestive language → Warning
  • Correct Channel Usage: Off-topic discussions → Warning
  • Argument Ignition: Heated discussions → Warning + 2-hour mute

Low severity offenses are typically addressed with warnings and educational messages.

Medium Severity Offenses

  • Spamming / Mass Pinging → Warning + 1-hour mute
  • Voice Chat Abuse → Warning + 1-hour mute if repeated
  • Advertising → 3-day ban (repeat = permanent ban)

Medium severity offenses require immediate action and documentation in moderation logs.

High Severity Offenses

  • Doxing / Harassment → Permanent Ban
  • NSFW Content → Permanent Ban
  • Treason Against USAF → Permanent Ban

High severity offenses require immediate reporting to senior staff and potential legal action.

Section 3 — Moderation Team Quota

Moderators

  • 3 valid moderation logs OR 200 messages every 2 weeks
  • Must maintain active participation in server activities
  • Required to attend monthly training sessions

Senior Moderators

  • 2 valid moderation logs OR 100 messages every 2 weeks
  • Must lead at least one training session per quarter
  • Required to review and approve ban requests

Community Staff Members

  • 1 QOTD every 2 weeks OR 2 events every 2 weeks
  • Required to maintain positive community engagement
  • Must attend bi-weekly staff meetings

Section 4 — Responsibilities of Each Moderation Rank

Moderators

  • Enforce rules, issue warnings/mutes/bans
  • Log all actions
  • Assist users with server rules

Senior Moderators

  • Perform all Moderator duties
  • Handle serious offenses
  • Oversee Moderation Watchlist

Community Staff Members

  • Engage with the community
  • Host QOTDs & events

Section 5 — Conduct and Expectations

  • Maintain professionalism and confidentiality
  • Enforce rules fairly and consistently
  • Respect the Chain of Command

Section 6 — Public Engagement Guidelines

  • Use a neutral and respectful tone when addressing disputes
  • Avoid public arguments; escalate issues properly
  • Do not discuss internal moderation decisions publicly

Section 7 — Moderation Logs

  • Username and Discord ID
  • Rule broken
  • Action taken
  • Moderator's name

Section 8 — Voice Channel Policies

  • Enforce rules to prevent disruption
  • Only join private VCs when responding to reports
  • Record VC activity if dealing with serious cases

Section 9 — Underaged User Policies

  • Immediately report users suspected to be under 13
  • Enforce Discord Terms of Service without exception

Section 10 — Raids & Admin Abuse

Raids

  • Mute or ban involved users
  • Notify upper staff
  • Document actions

Admin Abuse

  • Collect and document evidence
  • Report to the highest-ranking available staff
  • Maintain professionalism

Section 11 — Additional Guidelines

  • Confidentiality: Keep all moderation matters private
  • Escalation: Consult Senior Moderators or Admins when unsure
  • Training: Attend required training sessions
